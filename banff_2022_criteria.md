# Banff 2022 Classification for Renal Transplant Pathology

## Overview
The Banff Classification is the international consensus classification for pathology reporting of biopsies from solid organ transplants. The 2022 update includes changes from the 2019 and 2022 meeting reports.

## Banff Lesion Scores
- **i** - Interstitial Inflammation
- **t** - Tubulitis
- **v** - Intimal Arteritis
- **g** - Glomerulitis
- **ptc** - Peritubular Capillaritis
- **C4d** - C4d staining
- **ci** - Interstitial Fibrosis
- **ct** - Tubular Atrophy
- **cv** - Vascular Fibrous Intimal Thickening
- **cg** - Glomerular Basement Membrane Double Contours (Transplant Glomerulopathy)
- **mm** - Mesangial Matrix Expansion
- **ah** - Arteriolar Hyalinosis
- **aah** - Hyaline Arteriolar Thickening
- **ti** - Total Inflammation
- **i-IFTA** - Inflammation in Area of IFTA
- **t-IFTA** - Tubulitis in Areas of Interstitial Fibrosis
- **pvl** - Peritubular Capillary Basement Membrane Multilayering

## Additional Diagnostic Parameters
- Acute TMA in the absence of any other cause
- Prior history of DSA
- Severe Peritubular Capillary Basement Membrane Multilayering

## Banff Diagnostic Categories
- Acute TCMR Grade IA, IB, IIA, IIB, III
- Active ABMR
- Chronic Active ABMR
- BK-Virus Nephropathy
- Borderline changes
