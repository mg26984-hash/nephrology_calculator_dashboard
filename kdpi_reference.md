# UNOS KDPI Calculator Reference

## Input Fields (All Required):
1. **Age** (years) - numeric input
2. **Height** - dual input: ft/in OR cm
3. **Weight** - dual input: lbs OR kg
4. **History of Hypertension** - dropdown:
   - NO
   - YES, 0-5 YEARS
   - YES, 6-10 YEARS
   - YES, >10 YEARS
5. **History of Diabetes** - dropdown:
   - NO
   - YES, 0-5 YEARS
   - YES, 6-10 YEARS
   - YES, >10 YEARS
6. **Cause of Death** - dropdown:
   - ANOXIA
   - CEREBROVASCULAR/STROKE
   - HEAD TRAUMA
   - CNS TUMOR
7. **Serum Creatinine** (mg/dL) - numeric input
8. **Donor meets DCD Criteria?** - dropdown:
   - YES
   - NO

## Notes:
- Race and HCV status were removed from KDPI calculation in October 2024
- All fields are required
- Output is KDPI percentage (0-100%)
